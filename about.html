About Vanguard Home Service

Built on Service.
Rooted in the Lowcountry.

Vanguard Home Service LLC was founded with a simple belief: appliance repair should be fast, honest, and done right the first time. We're not a franchise or a call center — we're a locally owned business that answers its own phone and shows up when we say we will.

We're factory-trained on all major brands, from everyday workhorses like Whirlpool and Samsung to premium brands like Dacor, Sub-Zero, and Viking. As authorized Premier Service Providers for Dacor and Speed Queen, we hold our work to the highest standard.

We serve the entire Lowcountry — from Johns Island to the Isle of Palms, from Summerville to Sullivan's Island. When your appliance breaks down, we treat it like it's our own.
